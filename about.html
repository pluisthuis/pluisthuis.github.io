Jimmy Richardson is a creative technologist currently living in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, but his location is neither here nor there.

He started his career with big design dreams, and eventually pivoted to dev in 2014. After bouncing around a few agencies, and spending 5 years creating frontend at the advanced Milwaukee digital studio Northern Ground, he made the personal decision to go independent in late 2023.

When Jimmy is not working, he is an avid Great Lakes surfer and musician. More often than not, he can be found chasing swell along the eastern Wisconsin coastline.

His former band, award-winning Milwaukee Americana titans The Midnight Purchase, have been featured on Radio Milwaukee, and have played live for the Milwaukee Film Festival and Northern Public Radio (WNIJ), among many others.

He did the van thing back in 2016 before it got popular on the 'gram. San Diego to Seattle, for a year and half, just passin' through. RIP Ford E250.

Nowadays, he lives with his girlfriend and 2 cats that he considers renaming his studio after on a daily basis.
